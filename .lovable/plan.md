
# Two Virtual Media — Agency Website

## Design System
- **Dark theme** with deep navy/charcoal background (#0a0a1a range)
- **Accent colors**: Electric blue/purple gradients, soft cyan glows
- **Typography**: Clean, elegant — large hero headings, refined body text
- **Effects**: Subtle glow orbs, gradient borders, glassmorphism cards, smooth fade-in animations on scroll

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Home
- **Hero**: Full-viewport with animated gradient background, headline "Where AI, Creativity and Media Converge.", intro paragraph, CTA button
- **Services highlights**: Icon cards with glow hover effects (3-5 services)
- **Case studies preview**: 3 cards with image placeholders, titles, and short descriptions
- **CTA banner**: "Let's create something extraordinary" leading to contact

### 2. About Us
- Company introduction with mission/vision in a split layout
- Creative philosophy section with elegant pull quotes
- Team section with placeholder avatars and roles

### 3. Our Services
- 5 service sections (AI Content & Creative Strategy, Digital Campaigns, Visual & Media Production, Brand Communication, Interactive Media)
- Each with icon, description, and subtle gradient card styling

### 4. Case Studies
- 3 placeholder case studies: AI tech brand, cultural tourism project, beauty brand campaign
- Each with title, description, image placeholder, and key outcomes/metrics

### 5. Contact
- Contact form (name, email, company, message)
- Business email placeholder
- Collaboration invitation text

## Shared Components
- **Navbar**: Fixed, semi-transparent with blur backdrop, logo + page links + CTA button
- **Footer**: Company name, nav links, social placeholders, copyright
- **Responsive**: Mobile hamburger menu, stacked layouts on small screens
- Smooth scroll behavior and fade-in animations throughout
